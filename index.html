Welcome to Neni Farms

Dedicated to raising healthy, happy sheep and providing quality products.

Your Home for Premium Sheep Farming

Neni Farms is a family-owned and operated sheep farm nestled in the rolling hills, committed to sustainable and ethical farming practices. We take pride in raising robust and healthy sheep, focusing on breeds known for their excellent wool quality and tender meat. Our flock enjoys spacious pastures and a natural diet, ensuring their well-being and the superior quality of our products.

Whether you are looking for breeding stock, high-quality wool for your crafting needs, or delicious, locally-raised lamb, Neni Farms is your trusted source. We believe in transparency and are happy to share our farming methods with our valued customers. Explore our website to learn more about our sheep, our products, and our commitment to excellence in sheep farming.
