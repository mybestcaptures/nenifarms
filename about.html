About Neni Farms

Neni Farms began with a passion for sustainable agriculture and a deep respect for animal welfare. Founded in [Year, e.g., 2010] by the [Family Name, e.g., Neni family], our farm has grown from a small flock to a thriving operation dedicated to raising exceptional sheep.

Our mission is to provide the highest quality sheep products while maintaining a healthy and natural environment for our animals. We practice rotational grazing to ensure our pastures remain lush and nutritious, and our sheep are raised without the routine use of antibiotics or hormones.

Our Philosophy

At Neni Farms, we believe that happy sheep lead to superior products. We are committed to:

  • Animal Welfare: Providing a stress-free environment with ample space, fresh water, and natural forage.
  • Sustainability: Implementing eco-friendly farming practices that protect and enhance our land.
  • Quality: Focusing on genetics and nutrition to produce the finest wool and lamb.
  • Community: Connecting with our customers and sharing our love for farming.
